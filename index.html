About us

Who we are

Wacel Enterprises Limited is a Zambian privately owned company established in 2001.

We deliver professional engineering services and industrial operations support, with a collaborative approach that responds quickly to client needs.

Our core business is Electrical & Instrumentation and automatic fire detection and suppression systems for industrial and mining clients.
