Charging Racks & Battery Supply

Power solutions to keep critical equipment and safety devices operational.

Charging Infrastructure

Wacel supplies charging racks designed for mining environments to charge cap lamps, cordless lamps, loco lamps, flash lights and other portable safety devices. Storage capacity can be added to lock away SCSR units together with cap lamps.

We also support supply of backup batteries and loco batteries used for electric locomotives and other mining equipment, including batteries used for electric forklifts and golf carts.

Capabilities

We support supply and configuration of charging and battery solutions aligned to your equipment fleet and operational needs.

Charging racks for portable devices

Charging racks configured to charge cap lamps, cordless lamps, loco lamps, flash lights and other portable safety devices.

Storage and lock-away options

Storage capacity and lock-away solutions to store SCSR units together with cap lamps where required.

Battery supply for fleet and equipment

Supply support for backup batteries, loco batteries, and batteries for electric equipment such as forklifts and golf carts.

Support

We help configure solutions aligned to operational needs and equipment usage patterns.
